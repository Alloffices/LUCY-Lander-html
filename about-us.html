Where it all began

AutoEntry is a solution by OCREX, a company which specialises in the development of OCR software solutions.

Accountants and bookkeepers had faced a dilemma. They realised that in order to improve productivity, cut costs and gain a competitive edge, they needed to automate manual data entry. However, whilst some solutions were available, they were often expensive and lacking in the cross functional capability to tackle the nuances associated with manual data entry, and in so doing, drive real efficiencies.

Brendan Woods, software developer and founder of OCREX wanted to resolve this challenge and meet the demands for an intelligent, yet cost effective automated data entry solution. And in early 2016, alongside a team of top technical talent at the Dublin HQ, AutoEntry was born.

Following an audit of competitor solutions and feedback from industry professionals, every aspect of the underlying software was purposefully designed to serve the needs of its users. Leveraging Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Optical Character Recognition (OCR), AutoEntry operates with speed and precision across a secure platform. Its intuitive suite of features drive unparalleled working efficiencies and can flex to process simple or complex data sets, with verified accuracy.

Since its launch, we're delighted that AutoEntry has become one of the fastest growing solutions in its space, with more than 150 new businesses added to our platform every day. The future is digital and with more businesses transitioning to automated cloud-based solutions, we look forward to welcoming many more new users in the months and years ahead.
